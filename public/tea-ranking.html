The Best Teas According to Me

My top 3 teas of all time.

Lapsang Souchong

A very smokey, nearly whiskey-like tea. For the "I need a drink" moments during the day.

Jasmine Silver Needle

A flowery, fresh, relaxing tea. Reminds me of having tea with my grandma.

Hojicha

A nutty, delicious tea. The equivalent of a liquid snack.
